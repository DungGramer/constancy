# Funding

Constancy is a zero-dependency, MIT-licensed library maintained in personal time. Funding pays for security audits, fuzz testing infrastructure, and sustained maintenance. Every contribution — one-off or recurring — helps.

## Ways to sponsor

- [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/DungGramer) — monthly or one-time, with optional tiers
- [Patreon](https://patreon.com/dunggramer) — recurring support
- [PayPal](https://paypal.me/dunggramer) — one-off
- [thanks.dev](https://thanks.dev/u/gh/DungGramer) — share of your team's budget based on dependency usage

## What funding enables

- **Security audits** — the 27-vector red-team audit behind v3.0.1 took ~40 hours
- **Fuzz infrastructure** — Jazzer.js × 4 targets, ongoing coverage expansion
- **SLSA 3 provenance** — verifiable supply chain on every npm release
- **Active issue triage** — response time under 48h on critical reports
- **Docs + i18n** — bilingual EN/VI site, maintained content
